# ⚡ Quick Template Guide - 30 Second Tutorial

## 🎯 3 Steps to Change Your Resume Format

### Step 1: Add Your Resume
```
Type, paste, or upload your resume in the Live Resume Editor
```

### Step 2: Click "Change Template"
```
Purple button at top-right: [✨ Change Template]
```

### Step 3: Pick a Template
```
Click any of the 20 templates → Done!
```
